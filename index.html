More Information

Jasmine Thai in Carrollwood , Florida is a Thai restaurant serving authentic Thai dishes including egg rolls, Pad Thai, and Tom Yum. Our quick service and clean and friendly atmosphere is perfect for lunches, on the go, and dinners with family and friends. Jasmine Thai has been severing the Carrollwood Community since 1982.
